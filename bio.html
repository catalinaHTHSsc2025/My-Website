Julia Coxen Biography

Julia Coxen is my aunt and one of the most extraordinary people I know. Currently, she is a college professor at West Point Military Academy in New York, where she teaches Systems Engineering to college students. At this school, students are taught military training as well as traditional school subjects. When they finish college at West Point, they are both a college graduate and an army soldier. Julia Coxen is a well known and respected teacher here. Julia is currently deputy head of the Sytems Engeneering Department. Julia went to University of Pennsylvania for her bachlors degreee and is a military graduate. She went to Comlumbia for her masters, and University Of Michigan for her Ph.D. degree. She is a very acomplished women and was among the top 50 finalists out of over 1,000 appicants in 2017 for a NASA astronaut selection. She has two older brothers and two kids.
